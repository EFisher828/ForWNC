For WNC, By WNC

We're a small, tight-knit team serving the many communities of Western North Carolina with honest, no-nonsense weather forecasts. Our primary mission is to help you know what to expect before you leave the house during high impact weather events.

We aren't ready to launch the forecasting side of ForWNC, but in light of Helene's severe impact on WNC, we've decided to utilize this platform for sharing post-storm analyses.

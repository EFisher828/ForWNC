This map displays the current status of the sections of the Blue Ridge Parkway.

Data is sourced directly from the National Park Service's (NPS) list of current road closures. On average, the NPS updates the list every morning; however, ForWNC is not responsible for any sudden openings or closures that are not updated by the NPS. In addition, ForWNC is not affiliated with the NPS in any way.

On occasion, the NPS will reconfigure their webpage, causing this map to break. Direct all questions to Evan Fisher (@EFisherWX) on Twitter/X.
